🚨 The Problem · Slide 2 of 6

Why This Series Exists — The Hidden Crisis in Application Security

📖 Every application you build, deploy, or operate runs cryptographic components — algorithms that protect passwords, certificates that secure connections, protocols that encrypt data in transit. Most organizations have no idea what those components are, which are outdated, which have known vulnerabilities, or which are about to expire. This series teaches you how to fix that.

💀

Weak Encryption in Production

MD5, SHA-1, DES — algorithms broken years ago — still silently run inside millions of production applications. Without visibility, teams can't even detect the risk.

⏰

Expired Certificate Outages

A certificate expires at 2 AM. The payment gateway goes down. Hundreds of thousands in revenue lost. The fix takes 4 hours because nobody knew the cert existed.

🔍

Zero Cryptographic Visibility

Your Docker image ships OpenSSL 1.1.1 with a known Critical CVE. You never explicitly installed it. Your team doesn't know it's there. Attackers do.
